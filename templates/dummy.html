What is CRM?
{% comment %} {% endcomment %}

Customer Relationship Management (CRM) is a strategic approach that businesses employ to manage interactions and relationships with both current and potential customers. It encompasses various tools, technologies, and methodologies aimed at streamlining processes, analyzing customer data, and improving overall customer satisfaction and loyalty. CRM systems typically centralize customer information, allowing businesses to track interactions across multiple channels such as email, phone, social media, and in-person interactions. By leveraging CRM, organizations can gain insights into customer behaviors, preferences, and needs, enabling personalized communication and targeted marketing efforts. Ultimately, CRM facilitates better customer engagement, retention, and ultimately, business growth.
